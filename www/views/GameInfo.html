About Playwin Keno

  • Jackpot Rs. 21 Lacs.
  • Ticket MRP - Rs. 13/-
  • Draw Frequency: Monday - Sunday

Draw Details

  • The Keno Game has a matrix of 80 numbers.
  • On each draw date, there will be a random drawing of 20 numbers from a range of 1-80
  • To participate in a Keno game, a player can select 2 up to 10 numbers from a range of 1-80.Each such set is called a "SPOT"
  • There are 9 spots available for play to the customer, ranging from Spot 2 to Spot 10.
  • You have the option to play multiple games for each spot selected, i.e. 1/2/5 or 10 games. Each KENO Game is priced at Rs.13.
  • If all the numbers in your chosen spot match the numbers drawn for the day in any order, you win the prize as per the pre-announced prize structure.

Allocation of Prize Money

  • The State determines a prize structure for each game, the text of which may be obtained from the Sikkim State lottery office and at points of sale as well. The amount of each prize as a result of the draw is calculated in accordance with the prize structure as determined by the Government of Sikkim
  • The maximum prize liability on a single draw will be restricted to Rs.60 Lakhs on Match 10/10, 9/9 and 8/8. In case the total prize payout for each of the above prize levels exceeds Rs.60 Lakhs, the amount of Rs.60 Lakhs will be distributed equally amongst the winners in that category.
